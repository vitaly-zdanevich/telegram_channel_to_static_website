```
mkdir aaa/bbb/ccc
cp f aaa/bbb/ccc
```

Can be simpler with the `install` command:  
`install -D f aaa/bbb/ccc`  

And even with integrated `chmod`:  
`chmod 644 f`  

With a single command:  
`install -Dm644 f aaa/bbb/ccc`  

<https://linux.die.net/man/1/install>
